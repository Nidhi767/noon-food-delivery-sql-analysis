# Key Business Insights

## Customer Acquisition

- Daily new customer acquisition trends help identify periods of strong growth.
- Promo codes are an important driver of first-time orders.
- A meaningful percentage of customers are acquired organically without discounts.

## Customer Retention and Churn

- Customers with no orders in the last 7 days can be classified as recently inactive.
- Customers acquired more than one month ago but now inactive are strong candidates for re-engagement campaigns.
- Triggering personalized messages after every third order can improve retention.

## Promo Code Analysis

- Some customers use promo codes on every order, indicating heavy reliance on discounts.
- Organic customers may exhibit stronger long-term value because they are not discount-driven.

## Restaurant Performance

- Top-performing restaurants differ by cuisine type.
- Ranking restaurants within each cuisine highlights category leaders and underperformers.

## Customer Behavior

- Many customers place only one order, indicating opportunities to improve repeat purchase rates.
- Repeat ordering behavior can be leveraged for targeted loyalty programs.

## Strategic Recommendations

1. Send retention offers to customers inactive for more than 7 days.
2. Trigger personalized campaigns after every third order.
3. Reduce promo dependency by nurturing organically acquired users.
4. Partner more closely with top-performing restaurants in each cuisine.
5. Analyze one-time customers to improve second-order conversion.
